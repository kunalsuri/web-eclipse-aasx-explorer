Fixed in `shared/validation-rules/aasd-advanced-constraints.ts`; the old mislabeled logic (Blob MIME-type check, RelationshipElement first/second check) was dropped rather than re-tagged, since neither corresponds to any real `AASd-*` ID. Tests: `tests/unit/shared/validation/aasd/aasd-advanced.test.ts`, `tests/integration/validation/aasd-advanced-integration.test.ts`. Commits `f32ebe0`, `4996c44`; `ai/lab/WORKLOG.md` row `W-021`. |
